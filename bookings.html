De Weydeman Bar

The hotel features a charming in-house family bar, rich with unique antiques and history. Known as the De Weydeman Bar, it honors the legend of the De Weydeman Ox, a delivery ox that once carried the post to Rhodes. The ox passed away in front of the hotel, and its horns now serve as a centerpiece in this historic bar -ask the locals for the full story

Every art piece, every Mauser on the wall, and the old ski boots in our bar and dining room carry a story—some dating back to 1888, the year the hotel was built.

This is one of our front-facing rooms, featuring a private patio. Each of our rooms is unique, with its own distinct colors, style, and atmosphere, all tied together by the timeless charm of old-school elegance.
