CAMPING SITES

If you enjoy fly fishing in the renowned Bell River, hiking through scenic mountains, or discovering the rich biodiversity of mountain flowers and birdlife, you'll love our camping sites! We currently offer two stunning venues. For more information, feel free to reach out to us via WhatsApp or call us at +27 65 651 3837, or email us at info@rhodeshotel.co.za.

Southern Drakensberg

Unwind and immerse yourself in the serene beauty of the lush green pastures of the Southern Drakensberg.

Longholm Cave

One of our camping sites is on Longholm Farm, in the Maartenshoek valley, approximately 7km from Rhodes village. On this farm there's also a large natural cave that can be used to host functions of up to 300 people. This cave also has small drawings from the Khoe-Sān which indicate that they used to live and hunt in this area.

Braai by the River 

Nothing compares to the joy of savoring stokbrood—South Africa’s beloved bread roasted over an open fire on a skewer—paired with a hearty braai by the cool, crystal-clear mountain streams.
